Sathi Mere – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Sathi Mere. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and store your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Name, Age, Gender, Profile Photo
  • Email / Phone Number (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Location (City/State if provided by you)
  • Likes, Filters, Preferences
  • Chat Requests, Chat Messages
  • Wallet Balance, Recharge History
  • Referral Code & Referral Bonus Logs

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Matching & connecting you with opposite-gender profiles (as per your settings)
  • Sending Likes, Chat Requests, and managing chats
  • Wallet balance, recharge, bonus & transaction tracking
  • Referral system verification
  • Improving app performance & security

3. Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored safely on Google Firebase Servers. We use:

  • Firebase Authentication to verify users
  • Firestore Database for storing profiles, likes, chats, wallet & referrals
  • Encrypted communication between your device and Firebase

4. Sharing of Information

We DO NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties.

We only share information in cases like:

  • Legal requirement or law enforcement
  • To prevent fraud or harmful activities
  • To protect the safety of our users

5. Your Choices

  • You can edit your profile anytime
  • You can delete your account permanently
  • You can control who can message you
  • You can manage app permissions anytime

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is strictly for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below 18.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating this page.
